# OTel Architecture

OpenTelemetry is a collection of APIs, SDKs, and tools for generating, collecting, and exporting telemetry data.
Its architecture is designed around a few core principles: vendor neutrality, separation of concerns, and a unified
approach to the three pillars of observability — traces, metrics, and logs.
