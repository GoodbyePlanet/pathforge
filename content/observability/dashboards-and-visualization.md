# Dashboards and Visualization

Dashboards turn raw telemetry into visual representations that help teams monitor system health, spot anomalies, and investigate incidents.

## Key Topics

- **[[grafana]]** — building dashboards with Grafana
- **[[effective-dashboards]]** — dashboard design principles

## Core Idea

Collecting telemetry data is only half the story. Without clear, well-designed visualizations, teams drown in data instead of gaining insight. Good dashboards answer specific questions and guide incident response.
