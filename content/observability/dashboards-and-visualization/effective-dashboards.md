# Effective Dashboards

An effective dashboard answers a specific question at a glance. Key principles: limit to 5-7 panels, use consistent time ranges, put the most critical signals at the top, use thresholds and color to highlight anomalies, and design for the audience — an oncall dashboard differs from a business metrics dashboard.
