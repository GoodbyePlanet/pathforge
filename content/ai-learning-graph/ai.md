# AI

Artificial Intelligence is the field of computer science focused on building systems that can perform tasks that typically require human intelligence — reasoning, learning, perception, and decision-making.

## Where to Start

AI is a broad field. The best entry points depending on your background:

- **[[machine-learning]]** — the core engine behind most modern AI systems
- **[[natural-language-processing]]** — AI applied to text and language
- **[[large-language-models]]** — understanding how LLMs work under the hood
- **[[prompt-engineering]]** — working effectively with language models
- **[[ai-agents]]** — building autonomous systems that act in the world
- **[[rag]]** — enhancing LLMs with external knowledge retrieval
- **[[using-llms-via-api]]** — integrating LLMs into applications through APIs

## Why Learn AI Now

AI has shifted from academic research to practical infrastructure.
Understanding these foundations helps you build better products, work alongside AI tools more effectively,
and reason about what the technology can and cannot do.
